Frequently Asked Questions

🌸 How long do the flowers stay fresh?

Our flowers stay fresh for 5–7 days with proper care. We also provide flower food and care instructions with every order.

🚚 Do you offer same-day delivery?

Yes! Orders placed before 2 PM qualify for same-day delivery within city limits.

💌 Can I add a personalized message?

Absolutely. You can include a custom message during checkout, and we’ll handwrite it on a beautiful card.

📍 Where do you deliver?

We deliver all across the city and nearby towns. You can check serviceability by entering your pin code during checkout.

🎁 Do you offer gift packaging?

Yes, premium gift packaging is available at an extra charge. Choose from boxes, baskets, or eco-wraps.
